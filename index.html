Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2026

The MoneyTracker is a personal finance application developed by Mehul Panchal. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

1. Data Collection

The MoneyTracker does not collect any personal data.

We do not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Financial account information
  • Usage analytics
  • Any data linked to your identity

2. Local Data Storage

All data you enter in the app, including expenses, income, categories, and budgets, is stored locally on your device.

This data is never transmitted to our servers, and we do not have access to it.

3. iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud Sync, your data may be securely stored and synchronized using Apple iCloud so it can be accessed across your Apple devices.

All iCloud data is managed by Apple and governed by Apple’s privacy policies. We do not view, access, or store your iCloud data.

4. Analytics, Tracking, and Advertising

The MoneyTracker:

  • Does not use analytics services
  • Does not track users
  • Does not display advertisements
  • Does not use third-party tracking SDKs

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties.

Any data export or sharing occurs only when you explicitly choose to do so using features provided within the app.

6. Children’s Privacy

The MoneyTracker does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

7. Your Control Over Data

You have full control over your data at all times.

  • You can delete the app to remove all locally stored data
  • You can enable or disable iCloud Sync from your device settings

8. Security

We rely on Apple’s iOS security and iCloud infrastructure to help protect your data stored on your device and in iCloud.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
